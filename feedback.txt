### 🧐 Phase 1-3 Post-Fix Feedback (Updated)

This feedback reflects the latest improvements applied to the foundation, vision, and testing phases.

---

#### 🏗️ Phase 1: Core Architecture & Mocking
*   **Strengths & Improvements:**
    *   **Filter Responsiveness:** The addition of the **"Soft Reset"** logic for Out-of-Range (OoR) values in the `FusionEngine` is a major improvement. It ensures that when an object re-enters the field of view after a long absence, the moving average doesn't lag due to stale OoR values.
    *   **Config Centralization:** New constants like `TOF_OUT_OF_RANGE_CM` and `OOR_SOFT_RESET_COUNT` are properly externalized, keeping the logic generic.

---

#### 👁️ Phase 2: Vision AI Integration
*   **Strengths & Improvements:**
    *   **Performance Optimization:** The `OpenCVCamera` now intelligently skips software resizing (`cv2.resize`) when the hardware output matches the requested resolution. This directly fulfills **Performance Mandate #1** (lightweight operations).
    *   **Worker Reliability:** The Vision worker's error handling and re-initialization logic (in `main.py`) provide a robust safety net for physical camera disconnects.

---

#### 🧪 Phase 3: Testing & Validation
*   **Strengths & Improvements:**
    *   **Longevity Verification:** The introduction of `tests/test_longevity.py` (now totaling 72 passing tests) demonstrates a high level of engineering maturity. Validating system behavior over extended mock runs is critical before moving to physical RPi deployment.
    *   **Test Suite Depth:** Coverage of boundary conditions for both fusion logic and FPS fallback ensures that the "Critical" requirements in `GEMINI.md` are empirically verified.

---

**Summary:** Phases 1-3 are now in a **"Production-Ready"** state for the Mock environment. The codebase is highly optimized, stable, and extensively tested. The project is fully prepared for **Phase 4 (Hardware Integration)**.
